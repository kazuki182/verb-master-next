# QUALITY_AUDIT_V95

## Version
Ver.95 - Verb Quality Batch 4

## Target verbs
- open
- close
- let

## Reason for selecting these verbs
After previous batches improved start/show/run, leave/move/bring, and hold/set/change, the next sparse area was the group around open/close/let. Their basic meanings were already relatively developed, but the common expressions and phrasal-verb sections still had only one example per item.

## Agreed quality order
1. Basic verb usages
2. Idioms / common expressions
3. Phrasal verbs
4. Premium daily examples later
