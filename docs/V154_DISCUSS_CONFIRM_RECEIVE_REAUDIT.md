# V154 DISCUSS / CONFIRM / RECEIVE 再監査

対象動詞: DISCUSS / CONFIRM / RECEIVE

## 方針
- カテゴリは「基本」「句動詞」のみ
- collocations は空に整理
- 基本の focus は動詞のみ
- 句動詞の focus は動詞 + 前置詞/副詞まで
- 目的語は赤文字対象外
- discuss about は除外
- RECEIVE のコアイメージは「外部・相手 → 自分・会社側」

## 句動詞

### DISCUSS 5個
- discuss with
- discuss among
- discuss over
- discuss together
- discuss in

### CONFIRM 6個
- confirm with
- confirm by
- confirm in
- confirm on
- confirm through
- confirm against

### RECEIVE 6個
- receive from
- receive by
- receive into
- receive with
- receive through
- receive at
